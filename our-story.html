Our Story

How a small idea about rhythm changed the way we play.

Why Percoustix?

Percoustix began with a simple question: what if your guitar could carry the groove as naturally as it carries the melody? We set out to build a small, elegant solution that blends with how musicians already play — no awkward rigs, no learning new techniques. Just rhythm that responds to your hands.

Our approach

We design for feel first. The pads, firmware, and software are created to be subtle, responsive, and musical. Our design philosophy favors low-latency performance, simple workflows, and an honest signal path that lets your guitar remain the central voice.

The team

A few builders, players, and obsessive tinkerers — all united by a love of grooves and guitars. We collaborate closely with musicians during prototyping, so every iteration is driven by real playing, not spreadsheets.

Where we’re headed

We’re focused on making tools that inspire creativity. Expect more compact form factors, new sound mappings, and tighter integration with pedals and DAWs. If you’d like to follow the journey, join our mailing list for early access and updates.
