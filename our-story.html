Our Story

HEY KID, YOU'RE NOT GOING TO UNDERSTAND THIS NOW, BUT YOU WILL LATER ON. I HATE TO TELL YOU THIS BUT THOSE DREAMS YOU HAVE OF BEING A ROCKSTAR IN A BAND WILL NEVER COME TRUE. I KNOW YOU BADLY WANT IT BUT SOMETIMES DREAMS AREN'T ENOUGH THE TRUTH IS, ALL OF US ARE MADE FOR MORE THAN WHAT WE CAN IMAGINE. YOU'LL STRUGGLE IN SCHOOL, YOU'LL BE FRUSTRATED IN EVERY JOB YOU TAKE, AND THE FUTURE WILL NOT BE AS EXCITING AS IT'S PORTRAYED IN THE MOVIES. YOU'LL LOSE YOUR DAD AND THE WORLD WILL SHUT DOWN. AND JUST WHEN IT SEEMS LIKE YOU'VE LOST EVERYTHING YOU'LL MAKE A FRIEND. THIS GUY. IT WON'T BE A BAND THAT YOU GUYS START TOGETHER, IT WILL BE A COMPANY. YOU GUYS WILL INVENT MUSIC GEAR THE WORLD HAS NEVER SEEN BEFORE AT A TIME WHEN TECHNOLOGY IS REPLACING HUMAN ABILITY. THE WORLD WILL BE GOING IN THE WRONG DIRECTION. INSTEAD OF TECHNOLOGY BEING USED AS A TOOL, PEOPLE WILL BE USED AS TOOLS AND THE TECHNOLOGY WILL BE CALLED "INTELLIGENT". BUT THE MUSICAL INVENTIONS YOU GUYS CREATE WILL RESTORE HOPE IN HUMANITY BECAUSE YOU GUYS WILL BELIEVE THAT PEOPLE ARE GOOD AND CAN ALWAYS CREATE MORE THAN THEY EVER CONSUME. THE MISSION STATEMENT OF YOUR COMPANY WILL BE: "TO CREATE EVOLUTIONARY MUSIC GEAR THAT UNLOCKS AND INSPIRES THE SONIC POTENTIAL OF HUMAN ABILITY." DON'T WORRY KID, BRIGHTER DAYS ARE AHEAD...
